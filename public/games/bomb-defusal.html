💣 HOW TO PLAY

// OBJECTIVE

A bomb is wired with 12 coloured wires. Some are traps — cut one and the bomb detonates. Your goal: cut as many safe wires as possible, collect multiplier winnings, and cash out before hitting a trap or running out of time.

// HOW TO PLAY

  • Enter a bet amount and choose a difficulty.
  • Press START ROUND — the bomb activates.
  • Each wire shows its multiplier reward if it is safe.
  • Trap wires show ??? — you don't know which until you cut.
  • Cut a safe wire → multiplier increases → press CASH OUT to collect.
  • Cut a trap wire → bomb detonates → bet is lost.
  • The countdown timer runs at all times — if it hits 0, bomb detonates.
  • After every round, trap wires are revealed in red.

// AUTO CASHOUT

Enable Auto Cashout and set a target multiplier. If you cut a safe wire and your current multiplier reaches or exceeds the target, you automatically cash out.
