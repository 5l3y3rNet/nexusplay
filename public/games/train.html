HOW TO PLAY

  • Pick a carriage (1–5) in either or both bet slots
  • Set your bet amount ($1–$500)
  • Press BET during the countdown window
  • Once the train departs, the multiplier climbs from 1.00×
  • Press EVACUATE to lock your multiplier before your carriage is struck by lightning
  • If you're destroyed before evacuating — you lose your bet
  • Both slots play in the same round — two bets, one train
