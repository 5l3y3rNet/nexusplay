HOW TO PLAY

Objective

🎯Move your catcher left/right. Catch falling chips to earn money. Cash out before you run out of lives or time.

Chip Types

🪙Value chips (¼¢ to $2.50) — add money directly to your winnings. Bigger chips are rarer and come with bomb escorts.
⚡×¼ / ×½ — multiply your total winnings downward. Avoid these.
✨×2 / ×10 — multiply your total winnings upward. Rare and fast.

Hazards

💣Bomb — only dangerous if you CATCH it. Catching a bomb loses round earnings + 1 life. Let it pass safely.
☠Skull — catching a skull loses ALL winnings + 1 life. Never catch it.
❌Miss a chip — missing any value chip costs 1 life. No money lost, but 3 misses = busted.

Lives & Timer

❤️You have 3 lives. Lose all 3 → game over, winnings forfeited.
⏱90 seconds per game. Timer runs out → you keep all winnings.
💰Cash Out anytime to lock in your winnings and end the game safely.

Bonus Rounds (every 5 levels)

🎰Every 5 levels you unlock a Plinko Bonus Round. Drop 3 balls manually — each applies a multiplier to your winnings.
📈Higher bonus rounds (L10, L15…) have bigger max multipliers but the same risk floor. The later the round, the higher the potential jackpot.
🤔After each bonus you choose: CASH OUT (take your winnings) or CONTINUE (play on for more bonus rounds).
RTP: 96–97% at optimal play · Provably fair · Bet scales all chip values proportionally
