HOW TO PLAY

The Goal

Place a bet, flip cards to multiply your stake, and cash out before a bust wipes your bet.

Multiplier Tiers

Common×1.05 – ×1.40  ·  68% chance
Uncommon×1.40 – ×2.00  ·  24% chance
Rare×2.00 – ×5.00  ·  8% chance
💥 BustBet lost  ·  30% per flip

Bet Limits

Minimum $1 — Maximum $500 per round.
Use the chip buttons or type any amount in the bet field.

Controls

  • SPACE — Deal / Flip
  • C — Cash out
  • 🔊 — Toggle sound

Tips

  • Multipliers stack — each flip multiplies the running total.
  • The Cash Out button pulses gold when you're in profit.
  • Confetti flies when you hit ×2.00 or higher.
  • Play chips only — no real money involved.
